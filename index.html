Self Harm

Feel like harming yourself? Please don't. You don't have to panic, you are not alone. Let us help you get better, you matter so much and so does your well-being. We care about you and we are proud that you are here to get better. Let's try some alternatives.

•Pinch yourself.
•Draw or paint red/normal lines on areas that you feel like cutting.
•Take an ice cube and press it on a spot you would cut.
•Bite a lemon or eat a chili drop
•Knitting, boxing, running, painting or anything you are passionate about.
•Squeeze a stress ball.
•Punch a pillow or scream into it.
•Rant about everything that is bothering you in "Burn It" section. Your secrets are safe with us.

You can express anything you want.
